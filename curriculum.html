Where I come from...

I started learning cybersecurity when I was 12, digging through every bit of information I could find online. But what really made me fall in love with this world was hands-on work on real systems. My first exploit came at 13, when I found a vulnerability in my school's website that let me access it as an admin. From that day on, I knew this was going to be my career — and my passion.
