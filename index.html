Who am I...

My name is Sp4d3. I am a cybersecurity student with an early and sustained interest in the field, having begun my studies at the age of 12. My initial focus was on offensive cybersecurity, where I explored system vulnerabilities and attack methodologies. One of my earliest practical experiences involved attempting to gain control over the examination platform used by my school, which allowed me to better understand real-world security weaknesses and the importance of robust defensive measures.

What is this...

This is my website. On this site, I test my coding skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This is the first version of my personal page, where I share my work, background, and contact information.
